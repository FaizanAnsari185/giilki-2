Founder's Message
“As a school administrator we want to deliver highest quality of education. But the challenges faced in several areas of school administration is keeping us away from core of Education. We tried several platforms from SAP, Salesforce, Education ERP’s etc, all of them help us gather large amount of unprocessed data. This is again adding additional burden on School teams to process data and plan action.
Giiki is designed to help teams with the action plan. Every data point will lead to a workflow that will continue until successful closure. And every workflow is designed and tested in several institutions before launching.
Let us focus on Academics and let Giiki handle the rest”
